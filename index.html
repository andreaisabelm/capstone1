About Me

A learner at heart, I have taken a new interest in the world of technology. I studied psychology as a way to understand human beings and every single career choice beyond that point has been molded by my desire to connect with people. This time around it is no different. I believe technology can be the link we need to unify people and in my particular case, it can be the instrument with which I can make a difference in my country. I call technology, the great equalizer. In a country where most jobs require a specific journey in which networking can only be counted if it's a close friend or family member, the tech world demands openness. When looking for qualified people we open ourselves to meet people that are not from our background. In this way I hope to unify my passions for teaching, learning and people by learning programming and finding a way to educate Dominicans don't have access to the resources I do. Follow me as I take on this journey of Psycholpogist/Educator to Software Engineer. From 0 to Junior
